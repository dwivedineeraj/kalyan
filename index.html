What are the different kinds of On Arrival Visa?

All 'On Arrival Visas' are Tourist Visas. They have different durations (15, 30, 90 days).

I need Tourist Visa with Multiple Re entry facility. What can I do?

Multi-entry visas can be obtained from Nepalese diplomatic missions abroad or may be granted by the Department of Immigration in Nepal upon request and justification.
